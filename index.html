About Me

I'm a junior front-end developer and UX/UI designer. I love creating new experiences and pushing the boundaries of conventional design—you’ll see a bit of this in my projects.

Beyond coding and design, I’m passionate about video games and interactive media. Through them, I’ve come to understand the power of narrative, visual language, and gamification—elements I love incorporating into my work to create deeper user connections and encourage more active, intentional engagement. I believe that capturing and guiding the user's attention effectively is one of the most crucial aspects of web design and development.

For me, design is not just about aesthetics; it’s about crafting meaningful interactions that make every experience more intuitive, immersive, and enjoyable.
